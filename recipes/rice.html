Rice

Description

A more basic recipe. More of a guide on how to make simple rice. For this you can use Basmati, Jasmine or any grain white rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup uncooked rice
  • 1.1 cup or 300 ml of water
  • a rice cooker

Steps

  1. Thoroughly rinse uncooked rice under cool water in a bowl. Rinse by filling bowl with water, mixing the rice well in the water and then removing all the water. Alternatively, you could use a colander with small holes
  2. Once clean and all is water removed, the rice into the rice cooker bowl and add the 300ml of water
  3. Close the rice cooker and turn on as per rice cooker instructions
