Chicken Burger

Description

This time we will be looking at a classic of western cuisine. The chicken burger. The recipe will be focused specifically on making a fried chicken burger

Ingredients

  • chicken breast fillet
  • 1/2 cup of all purpose flour
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup of kentucky chicken mix
  • 1 burger bun
  • 1 leaf of lettuce/li>
  • 2 sliced pieces of tomato
  • 1 sliced piece of onion
  • 1 cup of oil for deep frying or semi-deep frying
  • other sauces and extras (optional)

Steps

  1. Cut 1 breast fillet in half so you have 2 shorter pieces and throw them in the flour
  2. Pour oil into a frying pan and turn stove to medium high heat
  3. Scramble the egg and throw the flour covered chicken pieces into the scrambled egg
  4. Now throw the egg and flour covered chicken pieces into the kentucky chicken mix
  5. When the oil is bubbling, slowly drop the chicken pieces into the oil
  6. Cook for 6-10mins. Ensure there is no pink in the centre of the chicken. Make cuts into the chicken if necessary
  7. Now butter burger buns and heat on another pan at medium high for 30 seconds
  8. Add your chicken pieces on a base burger bun followed by tomato, onion, lettuce and the top bun
  9. Enjoy!
